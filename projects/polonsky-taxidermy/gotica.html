Custom Taxidermy Services in Alberta

We provide professional wildlife taxidermy services across Canada, creating museum-quality mounts with exceptional attention to anatomy, realism, and long-term preservation.

Each project is approached with respect for the animal and a deep understanding of form, structure, and expression.

Specializing in Small Animal Taxidermy

While working with classic species such as deer, bears, and large mammals, our true specialization lies in the taxidermy of small animals.

These delicate works demand extreme precision and patience — a level of craftsmanship that few taxidermists are willing to take on. This is where our experience truly stands apart.

A Little About Myself

Hello, my name is Vladimir. I have been making stuffed birds, animals, and reptiles for private collections since 1987.

From 2000 to 2003, I also made visual aids and teaching materials for the biology department of the Kirovograd Pedagogical Institute in Ukraine.

From 2015 to 2018, I made and installed a zoological collection and dioramas in the nature department of the regional museum of local lore.

In 2018 and 2022, he completed an internship and assisted in the creation of Professor Vaha's zoological collection in the Czech Republic.

Currently lives and works in Canada.
